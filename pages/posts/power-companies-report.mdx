## Other improvements

- **Summaries in graph digests** all your digests now include a summary explaining what numbers you're receiving and how they're trending compare to the past, like this:
  ![Capture d’écran 2022-03-01 à 15.41.02.png](https://june-changelog.s3.eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/Capture_d_ecran_2022_03_01_a_15_41_02_50679a367f.png)
- **The accurate company level metrics** are now supported by the slipping away companies report. To make your company reports more accurate, send _groupId_ in the context of your track calls. [Learn more](https://segment.com/docs/connections/spec/b2b-saas/).
- We improved the speed of the activation report for companies with less than 2000 users
- We added the retention graph to the [Company retention](https://june.so/templates/company-retention) report
- The feature report and the slipping away reports can now be received in Slack
- We fixed as bug that was opening by default the property selector in the report setup
- We fixed a bug where the company cohort best cohort changed when clicking on "Load more.."
