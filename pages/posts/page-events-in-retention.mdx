If you like to use pageviews instead of track events to track user's behaviors, then we have good news for you:
you can now **use page views in 3 more templates**!

These templates are:

- [User retention ](https://june.so/template/retention)
- [Feature retention ](https://june.so/template/feature-retention)
- [Feature audit](https://june.so/template/audit)
