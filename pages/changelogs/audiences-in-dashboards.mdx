You can now add audiences to your dashboards.

Dashboards are becoming a great way to both share insights with your team, your customers and sometimes even for yourself to organize your own work.

This week we've added the ability to add audiences to your dashboards.

This is a great way to keep an eye on multiple audiences at once, for the team members that need to handle multiple audiences at once.

We've seen this being particularly useful to look at things like churn risk, health and overall usage across all the accounts of a Customer Success Manager.
