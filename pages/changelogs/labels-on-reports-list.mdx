If you have a lot of reports, then finding the right one can be painful.

To make your life easier we're excited to introduce labels.

Labels are a flexible way to organise reports in the way you want, whether it's by theme (activation, retention), team (core product team, success team) - or anything else really

Labels can be added when a report is opened:

![Screenshot 2022-10-05 at 18.14.15.png](https://june-changelog.s3.eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/Screenshot_2022_10_05_at_18_14_15_fb6b3c402b.png)

You can then edit them from the settings:

![Screenshot 2022-10-05 at 18.15.50.png](https://june-changelog.s3.eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/Screenshot_2022_10_05_at_18_15_50_057d071a4b.png)

And of course, you can filter your report list using them:
